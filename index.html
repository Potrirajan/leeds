ABOUT US

LEEDS Group was first established as Career Chart Consultancy in 1997. The business then expanded to form Leeds HR Solutions in Singapore in the year 2005. Headquartered in Singapore, our branch offices are situated in India, Bangladesh, Malaysia, Abu Dhabi and Brunei and is now a Singapore’s leading recruitment consultancy company.

Leeds HR Solutions honed its expertise for almost a decade in manpower training, recruitment, and manpower supply to industries such as Marine, Oil & Gas, Construction, Manufacturing, Health Care & Services.

To ensure high standards of in-house training and testing of selected skilled candidates, Leeds Skill Training Centre was set up in Chennai – India, in an area of 16,500 sq ft. Leeds Skill Development Centre was later started in Dhaka, Bangladesh, to enhance the skills of employees before they get deployed to client companies. Both the Training and Development Centres are accredited by ITE Education Services, Singapore.

In 2015, Leeds expanded into a group of companies with the establishment of Leeds Security Pte Ltd and Leeds HR Consultancy LLC. Leeds Group of Companies seek to be leaders in Manpower, Training, Recruitment and Outsourcing in Singapore and the Middle East.
